Privacy Policy

Introduction

Omneuroai ("we", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

By using the app, you agree to be governed by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:
  • Account Information:
    • Name and email address (e.g. Google Sign-In/Password, or other supported methods).
    • App usage and in-app purchase events.
    • Usage data for troubleshooting issues.
  • Device Information:
    • Device type/model & version.
    • OS (Android/iOS) & version.
  • Content:
    • Text input for text-to-speech conversion (processed only for generating speech and not stored after use).
  • Automatically Collected Data:
    • General app usage information, device events (e.g. battery level, language, and country).
    • Analytics info (e.g. session length, frequency of use).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
  • To provide and personalize app features and services.
  • To troubleshoot and improve the app.
  • To communicate with you about your account.
  • To process purchases and transactions.
  • To analyze performance & usage for improvements.
  • To enhance app security (e.g. prevent abuse, unauthorized access).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information in the following cases:
  • With Service Providers (for authentication, cloud storage, analytics, and crash reporting).
  • For legal compliance (if required by law or to protect users and the safety of users).

4. Data Retention

  • Account information is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Personal data (used for your account and in-app events) is stored only as needed for app functionality and legal purposes. Some data may be stored permanently, others removed upon account deletion or as required by law (see Section 5).

5. Security

We take industry standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure authentication. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the data.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app may interact with services such as:
  • Google Sign-In (for authentication)
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (used for crash and events reports)
These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for changes. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@omneuroai.com
